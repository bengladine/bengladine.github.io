Time Game

The Goal

Developing a game centered around Time Manipulation, transforming this concept into a puzzle-solving adventure akin to games like Portal. The idea was to manipulate time for specific objects to solve intricate puzzles.

The Process

Inspired by the movie Tenet, the concept revolves around reversing the motion of objects. This involved gathering time data (position, rotation, velocity) for objects and enabling their reversal in time upon user interaction. Check out the initial experiments below:

My Role and Insights

As the lead on the time manipulation mechanic, I focused on integrating this core concept into various gameplay elements, including weaponry and object interactions. This role also involved contributing to game design through brainstorming sessions, discussions, and testing.
