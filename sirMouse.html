About me

Sir Mouse: Costume Party

The Concept

Creating a mini-game based on the series Sir Mouse created by Fabrique Fantastique. We were tasked with creating a costume game where kids could go explore the world, solve little tasks and earn costume pieces! As a reward they could dress Sir Mouse the way they wanted to.

My Role

I was tasked with designing systems like navigation, save system, mini-game systems and interactables and also supervising other programmers working on other features of the game.
Taking active part of planning was also part of my duties. This was done with Jira.
