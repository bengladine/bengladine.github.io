About me

I'm an independent Game Developer, specialized in gameplay and tools development, based in Ghent, Belgium. With expertise in 2D & 3D games and VR, I offer comprehensive services from game design to game development, including project estimation and planning.

After graduating in Mathematics and Physics from Thomas More Mechelen in 2017, I shifted from teaching to game development and earned my degree with Cum Laude from Digital Arts & Entertainment, Kortrijk in 2020.

Since then I worked at one of Belgium's fastest growing game companies, Triangle Factory, Transfr Inc., it belongs to the very best companies in terms of the VR-E-learning business based in the U.S.A. Most recently, I worked at Fabrique Fantastique, also one of Belgium's finest animation series companies.

With Interstellar Ella: Maggie's Planetguide set to launch on February 9th on the Ketnet app, I'm excited to explore new opportunities and contribute to innovative projects!
